Student General Pass

  • Eligible for the Students above 12 years age, For city Students only.
  • Travel facility valid anywhere to anywhere within City/Sub-Urban limits.
  • Validity for 1 month & 3 months respectively.

Metro Pass

  • Eligible for all above 12 years.
  • Valid for all the general public for travel in all City Ordinary, Sub-urban and Metro Express Buses within the City & Sub-urban limits.
  • Validity for 1 Month tariff is Rs.1300.

Free Pass

  • Students below 12 years & Girls below 18 years can avail this pass for free.
  • free travel facility for One Up and Down, Valid for Ordinary Busses Only.
  • Validity till completion of the Academic Year.

Route Pass

  • Eligible for the Students above 12 years age.
  • This Bus Pass can be issued for the distance upto 4 KMs, 8 KMs, 12 KMs, 18 KMs & 22 KMs with concessional tariff.
  • Validity for 3 months (Quarterly).

What are the Payment Mode's Available?

We provide Online payment like Upi (Phonepe,google pay, paytm,etc), Internet Banking, Credit/Debit card.

How to Manage your Virtual Card?

You can manage your pass in your ticket page, you can easily see and update, renew the virtual card.

Can I use the TSRTC Virtual Card on all TSRTC buses?

Yes, the virtual card is accepted on all TSRTC buses, providing a hassle-free travel experience for passengers.

Is the TSRTC Virtual Card secure and protected against unauthorized use?

Yes, the virtual card is equipped with security features to prevent unauthorized use, ensuring the safety of passengers' travel passes.

What happens if my smartphone is lost or stolen with the virtual card on it?

In the event of a lost or stolen smartphone, passengers can login thier account in another devices and have their virtual card transferred to a new device for continued use.

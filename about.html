OUR VISION

The scope of the TSRTC Virtual card is extends to streamlining and digitizing the process of managing transportation passes within the Telangana State Road Transport Corporation. This includes providing commuters with a more convenient and efficient means of obtaining, renewing, and tracking their passes through online channels. The system likely aims to enhance user experience, reduce paperwork, and contribute to overall administrative efficiency for both the transport corporation and the passengers it serves. Additionally, it may offer opportunities for data analysis and reporting to improve service planning and delivery

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to make the old traditional tsrtc bus pass system to new digital and modern system , so the bus pass price will be reduced and we can use the bus pass hassle free without any tension.
